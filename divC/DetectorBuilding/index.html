Detector Building

Click here for rules.

Objective: Build a thermometer, using LEGAL components(see rules).
A legal component is an NPM thermistor. A Thermistor is just a temperature controlled resistor. You could use an Arduino to measure the resistance from the Thermistor, and use an equation to convert it to temperature.
Software Requirements:

  • Arduino IDE
  • Fritzing(Ok so apparently for some reason its not reaaally free, so... The link just gives you a zip lol.)
  • An OFFLINE Thermistor Calibrator
  • Github Desktop(Not really needed, but like... why not)
